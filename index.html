Kulture

Kulture is an app that helps international students submerge into the local and popular culture of any country. It's main purpose to help the users find cultural references like slang words, popular memes, music, art etc. of the foreign country they are in. It also enables translation and image recognition technology to effectively search for interests that they might not be familiar with.

PROJECT GOAL

A novel cultural experience in-hand and at the go

To solve for the user needs and pain-points, the goal of the app is to provide multiple mediums of learning to its users. While the main focus of the app is to act like a translator tool, it also provides value by discovering other cultural references that can help them along the way.

Beta concept

Translation and Word search

For the first iteration, I decided to focus on learning and finding slang words on the app. I wanted to test the waters to get further insights on user needs and requirements since culture can mean different for users. This concept focuses on discovering and searching slang words that can be translated into their language.

Feature Highlights: Kulture app

Onboarding

Kulture lets the user select the county and the language they wish to learn and translate in. It also asks them to select their interests to provide more relevant content.

Homepage

The homepage is customized to show different themes and trending topics. The user can search for a particular cultural theme or a word they are looking to learn about.

Explore

Users can explore content based on their interests. Each theme is translated in their chosen language to help them understand the cultural reference on their terms. They can also share the content that they like on social media.

Translate & Capture

Kulture provides the ability to quickly look for a theme in real-time through audio and image recognition. Users can simply capture an image or record any content to find translations and similar content.

Bookmarks

Each theme can be bookmarked and saved for later use. Users can build their personal dictionaries that they can refer back and use later.

next steps

Expanding the app to create a difference

To better cater to the user, content has to be sourced extensively in different languages. Further features like crowdsourcing content can be developed to make the app relevant and sustainable over time.
